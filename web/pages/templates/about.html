About me

I am an event/portrait/cosplay photographer based in Western Pennsylvania / Northeast Ohio area, who loves to capture exciting moments and recreating scenes from the original media.

My photo highlights this well while also showing a bit of my personality. I prefer using available/natural light, and my photography style is "clean and vibrant".

My gears consist of a variety of lenses, from versatile everyday zoom lens to old-school manual focus prime lens:

  • Nikon D750 with MB-D16 vertical grip
  • Nikon AF-S 24-120mm f/4g ED VR
  • Nikon AF DC 135mm f/2d
  • Nikon AF 50mm f/1.8d
  • Neewer Collapsible Reflector
